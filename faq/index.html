K1 official link vs alternative check

What is a K1 alternative page?

An alternative page may compare access paths, support routes, download risks, or H5 options. It should not hide the difference between a guide, mirror, and official route.

How do I check an official link vs a fake link?

Compare domain, page text, support path, app file source, privacy pages, and whether the link redirects through suspicious shorteners.

Is H5 online different from app download?

Yes. H5 access usually runs in a browser, while app download requires Android installation, permissions, version checks, and source validation.

Why compare support before registering?

Support visibility matters when login, wallet, OTP, or withdrawal issues appear. Weak support is a risk even when the page looks polished.

What makes a comparison page useful?

It should explain exact tradeoffs, list risk checks, link to evidence pages, and avoid pushing a single claim without context.
