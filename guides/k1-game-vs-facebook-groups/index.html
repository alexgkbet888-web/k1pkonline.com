Why compare guide sites with Facebook groups?

Facebook groups can surface screenshots and current chatter, but they often mix official links, copied bonus talk, and unverifiable support claims. A guide site is slower but easier to review page by page. Pakistan users should compare transparency, policy visibility, and link consistency instead of following whichever source looks more active.

Evidence to keep before the next step

Useful sources show how they separate facts, screenshots, and unknowns. A guide page should link into support, safety, and payment explanations. A group post should at least preserve context, date, and full screenshot path. If neither source does that, trust should stay limited.
